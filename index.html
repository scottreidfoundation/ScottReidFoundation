Registration

Get ready to swing into action for a great cause!

Join us on Saturday June 22nd, 2024 at Bay of Quinte Golf Course as we raise funds for local families facing cancer through the Scott Reid Foundation Charity Golf Tournament.

Your $120 per person entry fee includes green fees, cart rental, and a delicious dinner followed by an exciting live auction.

Registration through Bay of Quinte Golf Course will open soon. If you're interested in pre-registering, please send player fees via e-transfer to Connor.Reid.cr@gmail.com (and add a note with your first and last name).

About

The Scott Reid Scholarship Fund is a charity founded in honor of Scott Reid, a local Belleville athlete, beloved brother, uncle, son and husband that passed away in 2001 after a courageous battle with an aggressive type of cancer called astrocytoma.
